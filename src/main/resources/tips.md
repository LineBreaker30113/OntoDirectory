Use the Venn View to easily find files that exist at the intersection of multiple conceptual tags.
---
You can import entire directories at once. Onto Directory will automatically preserve your folder structure as ontological tags.
---
Right-click any file in the Files View to quickly rename its actual filename or export a copy of it to your system.
---
The Left Sidebar can be collapsed using the top arrow icon to give you a distraction-free view of your workspace.
---
If your Data Lake starts to feel sluggish after deleting many tags, click the 'Optimize' (Vacuum) button to defragment the database.
---
You can chain multiple AND / OR / SUBTRACT operators in the Files View filter to build highly specific file queries.
